Earth Works

Engineering works created through the moving or processing of parts of the earthâ€™s surface involving quantities of soil or unformed rock. The earth may be moved to another location and formed into a desired shape for a purpose. Much of earthworks involve machine excavation and fill or backfill.

  • Topsoil and deep excavation
  • Drainage or structure excavation
  • Bridge excavation
  • Channel and footing excavation
