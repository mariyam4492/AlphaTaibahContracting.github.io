About

Alpha Taibah Contracting Company is one of the established Contracting Groups in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Having vast experience in the construction industry and specialized in residential, commercial and industrial projects, we are offering our services in the fields of architectural, structural, electrical, mechanical and Information Technology (IT) on all levels -- in the public and private sectors. We are organized in three main divisions namely:

  • General Construction Division
  • Electro-mechanical Division
  • Information Technology (IT) Division

We have a highly trained and experienced team of technicians and engineers who have the on-site experience and skills to deliver the best workmanship to ensure that quality is never compromised.
